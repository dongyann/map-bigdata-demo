The key challenge the Insurance industry faces while growing its business with new offerings and expanded client base is an inherent risk associated with the underwriting and claims process. The risk control with underwriting and claims is a complex problem due to a variety of factors that include associated high research cost, incomplete or lack of information about new customers, and appropriate models for risk assessment. Therefore making appropriate decisions for the growth opportunity with new product launch, marketing, and new customer acquisition is a complex problem fraught with risk factors.

Insightzen has a suite of innovative products solutions to address the risk assessment and mitigation strategies with precision marketing by leveraging big data and AI driven analytic solutions at scale. These solutions provide Insurance industries the approach to balance risk while expanding their business operations.

 

Our core capabilities

Customer Insights

Customer 360

InsightZen AI platform ingests various internal and external data about customers at scale to derive customer profiles with a variety of features that include claims filing behavior, disease-based profiles, demographics etc. to create deeper insight about its customers for Insurance industry. A variety of AI based models are built to derive such Customer360 that we define as customer portraits that Insurance industries could leverage to make variety of business decisions.

Customer Segmentation

Based on customer360 profiles, customers are segmented into a set of categories aligned with Insurance business operations that could provide precisely directed marketing strategies to customize products aim on specific groups, improve customer acquisition. This asset provides a flexible customer segmentation tool to discover various segments based on state-of-the-art AI models.

Customer Insights

Customer insight represents value contribution of the customer to the company. Full cycle value insight predicts customers` preference, retention, profits and on with behavior data analytics to support decision making in marketing.
 

Precision marketing

Product recommendation

Recommendation engine provides the capability to recommend suitable products for each customer, generate well targeted insurance product recommendation based customer360 and customer segmentations through the recommender system.

Event marketing

Customer interaction with insurance product browsing, searching, and purchasing are collected for further analysis of customer behavior for personalization such as event marketing with detection of special dates, events, celebrations. These special events are utilized for highly targeted and personalized marketing strategies.

Personality marketing

The customization of product, contract, price and service effectively increases companies` capacity of customer acquisition and profit making. Personality marketing tool helps insurance companies to achieve highly customized and involved insurance sales based on personalized insights learned from demographic statistics, behavior preferences, life style features derived from a variety of social and interaction data.

Pricing

Pricing has strong connection with price sensitivity of customer, pricing optimization works to improve long-term customer loyalty to maximize revenue and profit. Pricing optimization model empower insurance companies to adjust price dynamically with data analytics advanced algorithm.
 

Risk control management

Fraud detection

A variety of time series and anomaly detection models are developed to alert fraudulent claims activities with very high level of accuracy. Both fraud detection and fraud prediction models are developed to help insurance providers get real time alerts based on a variety of patterns.

Risk evaluation

Based on patients claims data, patients are ranked based on the health conditions to assess the overall health risk. Such a health-based risk assessment may be leveraged by insurance providers to address risk factor in the underwriting.

Business and service innovation

Claim data insight

Insurance company, medical organizations generate massive data every day. Claims data insight enables insurance companies to get sales summarization and sales lead generation from massive amount of data, understand customer preference and segmentations for product and services personalization.

Claims prediction

Claims prediction model is based on state-of-the-art machine learning predictive models that provides guidance to insurance providers about future claims statistics that may be utilized to estimate company performance and balance sheet for future quarters. In the longer term, this asset may be utilized for product portfolio optimization.
